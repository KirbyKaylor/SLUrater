Report/Flag

Title: Report/Flag inappropriate behavior

Actor: any user

Prerequisite1: logged in

Prerequisite2: existing comment

Action1: click on a button of a smiley face

Action2: submit a form expressing the reason for flagging

Action3: confirm choices

Effect1: the comment is temporarily not viewable

Effect2: Admin is notified to review the report a determine whether to delete the comment or not

Next1: confirmation message that the admins have been notified

Next2: view page without the reported comment
